"Occlusion" Inspired D3.js Panel

About This Visualization

This D3.js visualization demonstrates temporal occlusion - how objects can be hidden over time by other objects in space. The circles represent data points from the CSV file, with larger circles potentially occluding (hiding) smaller ones that fall completely within their boundaries.

Temporal Structure Speculation: Occlusion represents a temporal concept because it shows how visibility changes based on layering order - what was visible at one "moment" may become hidden as objects are layered. This relates to how information architecture and spatial relationships evolve over time in design projects. For the precedent project this could be used to visualize the "pixels" made of potted plants and how they may have been overlapping in the digital planning process for the 20x20 image that was voted on.
