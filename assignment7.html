About This Poll

This poll collects data about users' dominant hand preference, categorizing respondents as left-handed, right-handed, or ambidextrous. It demonstrates real-time data collection and synchronization using Firebase, allowing multiple users to vote simultaneously while implementing rate limiting to prevent spam.

Potential Long-term Ethics of Data Collection: Even simple demographic data, like handedness, raises ethical concerns when collected over time. It can contribute to biological profiling or discrimination due to links with neurological and genetic traits. Users may consent to a poll without realizing the data could later be aggregated, sold, or used for unrelated purposes. Left-handed individuals, being a minority, risk being misrepresented or ignored in analysis. Once stored, such data is hard to delete and may be cross-referenced with other datasets, increasing privacy risks. Seemingly harmless data can enable subtle forms of discrimination in areas like employment or insurance. Ethical data collection must consider long-term implications and ensure informed user consent.
